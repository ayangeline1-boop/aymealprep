Gym Nutrition Tips

Concise, practical notes that match the tone of the app.

  • Hit protein evenly across the day instead of saving it all for dinner.
  • Keep pre-workout meals simple: carbs plus a little protein works well for most people.
  • Use low-sauce meals when you need clean reheating and less mess at work.
  • Repeat a few ingredients across meals to make prep faster and grocery shopping easier.
  • If a meal needs extra energy, add carbs first before adding lots of heavy fats or sauces.
